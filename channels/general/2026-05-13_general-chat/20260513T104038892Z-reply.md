# Cat

A small carnivorous mammal, *Felis catus*, domesticated for roughly 9,000 years. Sleeps 12–16 hours a day, purrs at ~25 Hz, and lands feet-first thanks to a righting reflex driven by an unfused vestibular system.

## Traits

- **Senses**: night vision ~6× better than humans; hearing extends to ~64 kHz.
- **Communication**: meows are mostly directed at humans, not other cats.
- **Hunting**: obligate carnivore; stalks, pounces, kills in under a second.
- **Social**: solitary hunters, but form colonies around shared food sources.

## Care

- Fresh water, high-protein food, a clean litter box.
- Annual vet visit; vaccines for FVRCP and rabies.
- Indoor cats live ~15 years; outdoor cats average half that.
